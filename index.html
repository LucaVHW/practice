What will you learn?

Soft skills

Today, employers are looking for tractors who are able to solve problems in an intelligent and creative way. That is why at BeCode we mainly teach you how to learn, how to work in a team, how to manage a project properly from A to Z.

Tech skills

At BeCode you will discover all aspects of building a website or mobile application. The training covers both "Front-End" and "Back-End" development. The Front-End, that is where the elements are built that you see on the screen and with which you can interact. The Back-End is the internal structure of the website or app.
